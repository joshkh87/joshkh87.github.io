My Story
Ever since I was a child, I have had a keen interest in technology.Throughout my youth, I mastered how to navigate PC applications though a MSDOS system, and by 10 years old I had build my own computer. At 13, I was exposed to HTML 4.01 (at which time i found my true passion). In 2019, I decided to do a deep dive into HTML5, and CSS3; to hone my design and implementation skills which you can see from my portfolio page. In 2020, with the looming uncertainty of the COVID-19 pandemic, I started to teach myself the preprocessors SCSS, SASS, HAML, and Jade. I use tool kits like Emmet to expedite my code writing. In 2021, I made the decision to start learning my first programming language Javascript. There is no turning back now. I spend 16 - 20 hours per week, learning and refining my Javascript skills through FreeCodeCamp and have moved into learning EcmaScript6 (ES6), snd javacript frameworks snd Python. I love this journey I am on, and would like to continue to refine my skills within the programming realm
Carer Objectives
My plan moving forward is to complete my classes on Javascript and move into Python along with mySQL (or even a completely different technology stack; no holds-barred, the sky is the limit for me).
